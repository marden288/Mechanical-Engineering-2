About Me

With over 15 years of dedicated teaching experience in Mechanical Engineering, I specialize in bridging theoretical principles with real-world applications to prepare students for successful engineering careers.

Areas of specialization include Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Machine Design, HVAC Systems, Manufacturing Processes, and CAD/CAM technologies.

My teaching philosophy emphasizes critical thinking, hands-on experimentation, ethical engineering practice, and lifelong learning in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Previously worked in industry on mechanical design and manufacturing projects before transitioning to full-time academia at Northwestern University and other institutions.
